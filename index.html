First They Must Fall

First They Must Fall is an experiential sandbox virtual reality game that tries to explore the connection between mankind and nature. You are the stabilizing force between the corruption of man and the impetus of the wild. You alone can bring about the cycle of growth and decay - cleansing the world of the obelisks and false idols of man and returning balance to the land.
